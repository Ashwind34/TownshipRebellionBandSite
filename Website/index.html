Meet San Diego's premier Rage Against the Machine tribute band, Township Rebellion! The group has been bringing high energy and a powerful style to local music venues since 2013. The band covers hits from the full RATM catalogue, including Bulls on Parade, Freedom, Bombtrack, and many others! Come see San Diego's newest tribute band at a local watering hole near you!
